01

Privacy Policy

At Aurelian Bespoke Homes, our commitment to excellence extends to the stewardship of your personal information. We treat your data with the same architectural precision and security we apply to the foundations of our properties.

I. Collection of Information

We collect personal identifiers during the consultation phase, including architectural preferences, financial qualifications for high-value construction, and contact details. This information is gathered solely to facilitate the bespoke design-build journey.

II. Data Utilization

Your information is utilized to refine architectural drafts, coordinate with artisanal contractors, and secure necessary municipal permits. We do not sell client registries to third-party marketing entities.

III. Security Protocols

Digital documentation of your estate plans and personal data is encrypted using military-grade protocols, stored on private, high-security servers to ensure absolute confidentiality.

03

Accessibility Statement

Exclusivity in our homes does not mean exclusion in our digital presence. We strive for a seamless, accessible experience for all visitors.

We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website by adhering to many of the available standards and guidelines (WCAG 2.1). Our digital gallery is designed to be compatible with modern screen readers and assistive technologies, ensuring our 'Meticulous Journey' is viewable by every aspiring homeowner.

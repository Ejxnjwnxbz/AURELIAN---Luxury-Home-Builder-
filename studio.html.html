FOUNDER & PRESIDENT

Alistair Aurelian

With over three decades of redefining the luxury landscape, Alistair leads Aurelian with a philosophy rooted in the timelessness of classical proportions and the audacity of modern minimalism. His vision is simple: to build homes that are not merely shelters, but legacies.

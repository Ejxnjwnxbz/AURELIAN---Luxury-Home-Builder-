The Glass Pavilion

location_on Bel Air, California

The Vision

Conceived as a habitable sculpture, The Glass Pavilion erases the boundary between interior sanctuary and the raw beauty of the Californian landscape. This estate is a testament to uncompromising modernism, where structural purity allows nature to become the ultimate interior finish.

Every sightline was meticulously calibrated to frame the sweeping vistas of Bel Air, while maintaining profound privacy. The interplay of shadow and light shifts continually throughout the day, transforming the living spaces into a dynamic gallery of natural illumination.

Materials & Craftsmanship

Sourced globally but assembled with local artisan precision, the material palette of The Glass Pavilion is intentionally restrained to emphasize form and texture.

  • Monumental Glazing

    Triple-pane, low-iron structural glass ensures optimal thermal performance while providing distortion-free clarity.

  • Architectural Steel

    Hand-finished brushed steel columns and fascia provide a dark, monolithic counterpoint to the transparency of the walls.
